Milkshake

Description

A classic creamy treat that indulges all the senses. This delicious shake starts with a scoop of rich and creamy chocolate ice cream, swirled together with cold milk in a blender. The smooth, velvety texture of the drink is made complete with the addition of some chocolate syrup to give it a luxurious flavor. The final result is an irresistible concoction that can satisfy any sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Ice Cream
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Place a scoop of chocolate ice cream in a blender.
  2. Pour in cold milk until the mixture reaches desired thickness.
  3. Blend the mixture until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add a few tablespoons of chocolate syrup to the mixture and blend again until combined.
  5. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass and top with whipped cream, if desired.
  6. Place a maraschino cherry on top.
  7. Enjoy!
