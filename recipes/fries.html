Fries

Description

A classic side dish and snack enjoyed by people all around the world. They consist of deep-fried strips of potato that have been cut into thin, elongated shapes. French fries are typically served hot with salt, ketchup, or other condiments.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil (for deep-frying)
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and peel potatoes. Cut them into thin strips about 1/4 inch wide.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove some of the starch and help them crisp up better when cooked.
  3. Preheat oil in a deep-fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Carefully add the potato strips to the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pot. Fry for 3–5 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  5. Remove from oil and place on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.
  6. Sprinkle with salt (optional) and serve hot with desired accompaniments.
