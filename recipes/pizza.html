Pizza

Description

A classic Italian-American dish made with a flat, round crust, topped with tomato sauce and cheese, and finished off with slices of spicy pepperoni. The cheese used is usually mozzarella or provolone. This type of pizza is flavorful and filling, and it's a favorite for pizza lovers the world over!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Basil
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the pizza and top with pepperoni slices.
  5. Sprinkle oregano, garlic powder, basil, and red pepper flakes over the pizza as desired.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let cool before slicing and serving.
